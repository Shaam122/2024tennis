About Tennis

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles).

Rules of Tennis

  • Players must hit the ball over the net and into the opponent's court.
  • The ball can only bounce once on each side of the net.
  • Points are scored when your opponent fails to return the ball in bounds.
